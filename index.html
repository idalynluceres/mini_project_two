OUR STORY

PROJECT365 WANTS TO THANK YOU!

A journey full of sacrifices and with all the strength of our soul, to help those in need. For any public cause, we have diverted to 100% of the collected fund. This is how we operate from our heart!

Thanks to honored and heartfelt Filipino's who did everything to help all the children. Thanks to all of you, as this was just the beginning … For 2022, we were awarded with the “Region Champions” award for South East Asia. We are very honored and we hope that this award will bring more light to the PH families lives. We called people’s hearts to join our mission. So far this journey has traveled miles away. Along with the items they bought, people gave a piece of their heart.
